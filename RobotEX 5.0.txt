<h1>Our Corporation</h1> 
<p1>Established in Mobile, Alabama, RobotEx has been a leader in the robotics industry.<br></p1>
<p2>Initially, as a medical technology provider for BEST Robotics, Inc.,<br></p2> 
<p3>RobotEx has expanded its enterprise to include a range of industrial and commercial applications.<br></p3> 
<p4>Our company is dedicated to the delivery of quality products, maintenance of a safe working environment,<br><p4>
<p5>and continued development as a good corporate citizen.<br></p5>
<h1> BEST</h1>
<p1>BEST (Boosting Engineering, Science, Technology) is a non-profit, volunteer-based organization whose mission<br></p1>
<p2>is to inspire students to pursue careers in engineering, science, and technology through participation in a<br></p2>
<p3>sports-like, science and engineering-based robotics competition.<br></p3>
<h1>Our School</h1>
<p1>Established in 1960, Davidson is a 4- year Highschool, fully accredited by SACS (Southeastern Association of Colleges and Schools).<br></p1> 
<p2>Davidson offers one of the most comprehensive programs available to students in Mobile, Alabama.<br></p2>
<p3>It became the first public high school in the county to offer a full-time gifted and Advanced Placement program.<br></p3>
